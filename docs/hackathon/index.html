2019 Computer Science Society Hackathon!

Posted on 2019, Apr 13 by Swopnil N. Shrestha 2 mins read

Welcome to the Luther College Computer Science Society Hackathon!

Please accomodate yourslef to the hackathon rules and the recources below.

This is a reminder that the 2nd CSS Hackathon will kickoff tomorrow, April 13th at 10AM in Olin 202. This is a great opportunity for you to gain experience working in teams and get projects/experiences under your resume.

Please arrive by 10AM and you can choose to join a team, form your own team or work individually on a project. This hackathon is open to all, so if you have taken a CS course or are currently enrolled in one, you will be able to participate!

While the hackathon will go on from 10AM to 8PM, you are not required to stay for the whole time and can take lunch/snack breaks are you please. However, you are required to be at the initiation (10AM) and project presentations (7:30PM). Snacks will be provided.

We will have exciting tech based prizes and awards will be for the following categories: 1. Best Overall in the Tournament 2. Runner Up in the Tournament 3. Best Computer Science 4. Best Data Science 5. Best User Interface 6. People’s Choice 7. Highest Business Potential

Rules for the Tournament * Anything that you work on today must be started here * You can use any framework * If you are using a tutorial, use must extend it * You may replicate a website * There can be maximum of 3 people in a group * All work must begin after 10AM and end before 7:30PM today * Your project does not need to be complete * Final presentations have to be limited to * 5 minutes for individuals and pairs * 7 minutes for groups of 3

Resources * MLH Hackathon Resources * Google Developers Godelabs https://codelabs.developers.google.com/ * Lynda https://lynda.luther.edu * GitHub Pages https://pages.github.com * Heroku https://heroku.com * GitHub https://github.com/xasos/Hackathon-Resources (and other repos)

We hope you are looking forward to a day of code. We definitely are!

All the best, Your CSS Executive Board
