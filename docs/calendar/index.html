Hackathon Sunday April 13th

Posted on 2019, Apr 11 by Swopnil N. Shrestha One min read

Happy Friday!

Hackathon is happening in one week! On Saturday April 13th, the day-long event will kick-off in Olin 202 at 10AM with team formation and project brainstorming/selection. Individuals and teams will then be able to spend the day working towards creating a functioning product. Project presentations (and prizes!) will be in Olin 202 at 8PM.

A few notes: 1. It is not required for participants to stay in the room the whole day, nor spend the entire time working on the project. Take breaks for meals, and certainly do a bit of classwork if needed! 2. The goal of Hackathon is to take a few hours and experience a sprint-like development style. This is a great time to start a personal project and for experience collaborating with other programmers. 3. There will be project ideas for students of all skill levels, so everyone can participate! 4. Snacks will be provided :)

Please head to the Hackathon Event Page on Facebook to RSVP for Hackathon. If you don’t have a FB account, you can also email us at css@luther.edu. Hope to see you there!

Sincerely, Your CSS Exec Board

P.S. Elections for the next CSS Exec Board will be coming up soon. Stay tuned for more info!
